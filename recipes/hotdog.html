American Hotdog


A hot dog is a dish consisting of a grilled, steamed, or boiled sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun.

Ingredients

  • 4 beef frankfurters
  • 4 hotdog buns
  • 1 small onions
  • 3-4 teaspoons sweet pickle
  • 1 cold-pack kosher dill picke
  • 1 small tomato

Steps

  1. Simmer frankfurters in beer or water for approximately 10 minutes.
  2. Warm buns in microwave, until slightly warm and soft.
  3. To assemble hot dogs, place frank in each bun.
  4. Add mustard to taste; top with dill spear, relish, onion, tomato, and 1-2 sport peppers
  5. Sprinkle with celery and poppy seeds.
  6. Serve.
